Hi,

I would usually make this type of request by personal email or telephone but I have been unable to
find a real person's email address to contact either on VORS, grid-cat or the public interface of the
umbrella UFlorida-Tier2 site.

DZero will be embarking on a large production run in December of approximately four months in
duration is is looking to maximize its use of available OSG resources during that time.

DZero's production jobs will have the following requirements:

1) Approximately 6GB of local scratch space on each node for temporary job data.
2) Outgoing network access from worker nodes to offsite servers.
3) A low (preferably zero) preemption rate for jobs of 12hours duration or less as measured on a
3GHz Xeon CPU

In addition, DZero is asking sites where possible for of order 1TB of network-available storage for
the duration of the run. If sites are amenable to this I can put them in contact with technical
personnel from DZero to handle specifics.

Thanks for your time and help,
