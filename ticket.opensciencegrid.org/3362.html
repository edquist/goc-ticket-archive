    Apr 10, 2007 08:11 PM UTC by Tim Silvers 
    Alan,

No other groups in OSG are currently using OTRS-based systems...at least, none that exchange
tickets with us. We use Footprints, which is a commercial product. Our ticket system requires that
you send a reply to the ticket system address with our ticket number in the Subject of the email,
"ISSUE=3444" for example. So updating our tickets is rather simple. However, our ticket system
sends acknowledgments to every incoming email. It does so in order to keep all Assignees working
the tickets, as well as the customer, informed. However, typically this means that a Support Center
like TIGRE is an Assignee, and if TIGRE's ticket system also sends acknowledgments, a mail loop is
created. Obviously, we need to be careful to avoid this.

It is possible for you to not only send us email updates, but to populate some of our ticket fields
with the incoming email. We can go into this if necessary. But I suppose the question I have for
you is, does your ticket system auto-reply to incoming email? What information would need to be
included in an email sent to your system in order for it to match the incoming email to an existing
ticket? Do you need a specific string or ticket number in the Subject?

Thanks,

Tim

    Apr 10, 2007 03:27 PM UTC by Tim Silvers 
    Thank you for registering the TIGRE Support Center. Your registration will be added to the OSG
Support Structure.

As a registered support center you should have a representative attend the weekly OSG Operations
Meeting to review issues facing your users and sites. You should also consider attending the weekly
meeting of the Support Centers Technical Group. Details for both meetings can be found at http://
osg.ivdgl.org/twiki/bin/view/Operations/WebHome

There are several mailing lists associated with the OSG, as a new SC you may want to subscribe to
the following mailing lists:

osg@....  -- For general OSG announcements
osg-general@....  -- For general technical discussion and open community support
osg-operations@....  -- Operations activities
osg-ops-status@....  -- OSG Status Reports
tg-supportcenters@....  -- Support Centers Technical Group

Details for subscribing to these lists and a list of other OSG mailing lists you may be interested in
subscribing to can be found at http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000089.

Please contact the Grid Operations Center (GOC) at goc@.... if you have any
questions.

Thank you for registering your support center, and we look forward to your participation supporting
the OSG!
