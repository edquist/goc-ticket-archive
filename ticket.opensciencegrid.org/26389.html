    Aug 14, 2015 03:14 PM UTC by Brian Lin 
    Doug,

Yes, you will need to be running a second schedd on your CE (one will correspond to your pool HTCondor and the other for HTCondor CE). If users are already able to submit from your central manager, this should suffice. If you have a small cluster, then having the CE on your central manager may be ok but once it gets to a sizeable number of jobs/cores, then it's pretty standard to separate out the schedd and collector/negotiator onto separate hosts. I'll close this ticket for now but don't hesitate to open another ticket if you're having more issues.

Cheers,
Brian
