    May 5, 2015 11:52 PM UTC by Matyas Selmeci 
    I see the issue. cemon did not require gip to run, but osg-info-services does. If (and only if) gip is installed, osg-configure will require a GIP section of the config.

You should install osg-configure-gip. That will give you /etc/osg/config.d/30-gip.ini; make the sc_number=0 change I mentioned below, and rerun osg-configure.

I made a JIRA ticket about the missing dependency: https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-1911
I also created a ticket about osg-configure handling SE-only installs: https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-1912

-Mat
