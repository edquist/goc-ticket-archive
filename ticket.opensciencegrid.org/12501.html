To register your site with WLCG

To register a new Tier site(s) within an existing Federation into the WLCG, you will need to contact LCG Office.

Please check that your Federation is listed in the REBUS Topology. Then contact LCG Office with the following information about the new site(s) you wish to register:

The Federation you are part of eg. "Brazil, SPRACE, SÃ£o Paulo"
The Accounting Name eg. "BR-SP-SPRACE"
The site name(s) - eg "SPRACE"

    Hi Elizabeth,

Vanderbilt is in a somewhat uncertain state at the moment with regards to interop accounting.  There are some organizational steps that need to be taken before we can resolve the technical steps.  The appropriate people have been notified to get things rolling.

In the general case (Vanderbilt's case isn't), the site needs to sign an MoU with the WLCG first.  Then they get added to the WLCG topology.  Once they are there, the Interop Accounting flag in OIM can be set.  Rob will verify at the next WLCG OPS call that what I have stated here is correct.
