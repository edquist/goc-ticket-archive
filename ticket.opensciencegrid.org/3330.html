Hi,

On Florida PG and HG clusters, we have observed that Dzero jobs are
being evicted by condor and restarted again on a different node. For
Dzero reprocessing jobs that we are running on OSG, eviction could cause
problems. If the job is evicted and restarted on a different worker
node, the job will certainly fail. Dzero jobs usually run for ~12 hours.
Preempting the job wastes the computing time used on the first node and
also wastes the computing time on subsequent nodes when it is restarted,
since, it is bound to fail on subsequent tries.

Is it possible to get dedicated time slot for the Dzero jobs so that
they are not preempted for atleast 12 hours of running time?
