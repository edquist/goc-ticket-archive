-- by Sebastian Pulido at Wed Mar 09 09:38:19 UTC 2016

Hi all,

reporting in ticket a summary of my private mail exchange with Wayne:

1) The "Could not submit to FTS" issue was caused by missing glite-transfer-* commands on the new T2_US_Caltech PhEDEx host. The new host is configured with the OSG 3.3 distribution which removed the old FTS clients, replacing them with the new FTS3 clients (fts-transfer-* commands).

As a workaround, Wayne defined the following aliases in the PhEDEx env:

    
alias glite-transfer-submit='fts-transfer-submit'
alias glite-transfer-status='fts-transfer-status'

2) After this change, the T2_US_Caltech download agent was unable to check the status of FTS transfers, because the output of fts-transfer-status is slightly different from the output of glite-transfer-status (e.g. "FINISHED" instead of "Finished"). Fixed applying this patch to T2_US_Caltech PhEDEx agents:

https://github.com/dmwm/PHEDEX/commit/5e3c69bbc65ef43d2b1c26d5d9e7013b9fbe190e

After applying this change, transfers to T2_US_Caltech in Debug seem to be working.
