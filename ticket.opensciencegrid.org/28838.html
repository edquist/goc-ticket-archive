Short Description: Resource naming/combining issue affecting WLCG accounting at Nebraska
Caller: cd-srv-goc-ops
Categorization:
Priority: 4 - Low

Summary: The WLCG reports (http://accounting.egi.eu/reptier2.php) for T2_US_Nebraska have been incorrect for the past few months after the HCC resources known as Tusker, Crane, and Sandhills were changed in Gratia to be known as "Nebraska-HCC" -- see https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/27831 for reference of that change.

This had the unintended consequence of causing the Tusker and Crane resources to disappear (report zero hours) in the WLCG reports it seems, I assume due to the topologies in MyOSG and the EGI REBUS system being out of sync. The folks involved with the renaming in Gratia were most likely unaware of the interaction with Gratia->APEL->WLCG accounting.

I think it would be easiest if we can simply have both the "Nebraska" and "Nebraska-HCC" resources in Graita end up reporting upstream to APEL and WLCG as simply "Nebraska". Can this be changed at the MyOSG/Gratia level? This would let us simply be "Nebraska" to the WLCG end and help end some confusion.
