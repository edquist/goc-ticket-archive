    May 25, 2013 08:11 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    FermiGrid is in a state right now where our worker nodes are at OSG 3.0 and our gatekeepers are not yet.
So you will have to explicitly use /usr/sbin/glexec instead of the OSG_GLEXEC_LOCATION which still points
to /opt/glite/sbin/glexec.  The former is from OSG 3.0 and the latter is from the VDT.  Only the OSG 3.0 one will
work with proxies based on Digicert service certificates like the one you are using.
